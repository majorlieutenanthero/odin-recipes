Description

The chicken pasta alfredo which is famous for its creamy and satisfyingly savory quality. It can be pretty hearty with our yummy fettucine pasta mixed with bits of chicken.

Compared to other pasta dishes. This dish is quick and simple to cook as we will only be mainly boiling and stirring the ingredients together.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams CDO Chicken Flakes in Oil
  • 1 lb. fettucine pasta
  • 500 ml. all-purpose cream
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 3 clvoes garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Set aside.

  2. Make the creamy sauce by melting butter in a cooking pot. Saut√© onion and garlic until the onion softens.

  3. Add CDO chicken flakes. Continue cooking for 2 minutes.

  4. Pour the all-purpose cream into the pot. Let it boil. Adjust the heat to low setting. Continue cooking while stirring occasionally until the sauce thicken to your desired consistency.

  5. Add parmesan cheese. Stir.

  6. Put the cooked pasta unto the cooking pot with the sauce. Toss until well blended. Season with chopped parsley.

  7. Transfer to a pasta bowl. Serve.

  8. Share and enjoy!
